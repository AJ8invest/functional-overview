# Buyer

An individual, or representative of an organisation who they are purchasing on behalf of, who is the buyer of Learning Material via a Marketplace.

## Example Types

  - Student (for VO+)
  - Teacher
  - Parent
  - Leermiddelen coordinator (LMC)
